Terms and conditions

Eligibility

  • Fastra Referral Programme is open to you only if you are a user on your organisation’s FastraSuite software or have been selected by our in-house team.

  • We’re defining referral leads as new customers to FastraSuite, so referrals who turn out to be one of our existing customers will not count.

  • Your referral lead is ineligible if it is a contact at a company that our Sales unit is already in discussion with. We will inform you about such a development as soon as we discover it.

  • No referral will be accepted from an employee of a Channel Partner.

  • The team will notify you if your referral lead results in a sale.

  • If you are an employee of a FastraSuite client company, your company must give you permission to participate.

  • From the provisions of the Code of Conduct Bureau and Tribunals Act, public officials/government employees are exempted from participating in this programme.

Rewards

  • The referrer’s earnings will be 10% of the total deal value and is capped at NGN 5,000,000.

  • 10% of the Implementation Fee payable 7 days after payment is received from the client.

  • 10% of the subscription fee payable 30days after the client has gone live on the application.

  • We will pay every referral when due into designated corporate and partner accounts.

  • Individual referral partners who are employees of Fastra Suite must ensure they abide by their company disclosure policies regarding collection of rewards.

  • The referral will also be time-based and the deal will have to be closed within 4 months of it being referred. Longer time may be considered for large-sized organizations.

Please note that referral rewards will only be paid once per referred account.
